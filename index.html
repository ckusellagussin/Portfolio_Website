About Me

Who I Am

Hello, My name is Casey Kusella-Gussin. As I am writing this, I am 29 years old and currently residing in London, United Kingdom. I have been in love with video games my whole life and I have been blessed to work in the video game industry for two and a half years with the hunger still there.

I went to the University of Plymouth and graduated with a BSc with Honours in Computing and Video Games.

My favourite video games either involve an intensive and urgent story like Mass Effect 2 and Knights of the Old Republic or a mind boggling set of game mechanics and systems such as RimWorld, Path of Exile and Crusader Kings.

My expertise lies in crafting responsive gameplay mechanics, optimizing performance, and collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to bring creative visions to life.
